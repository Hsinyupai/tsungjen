Tsung-Jen Pai 白宗仁

Born in Taichung, Taiwan in 1963, Tsung-Jen Pai graduated from the Fine Arts Department of Chinese Culture University in 1985. He furthered his education, earning a Master of Arts degree from the Graduate Institute of Art History at the same university in 1990, and a doctorate in Fine Arts from the China National Academy of Arts in Beijing.

His academic career includes serving as a professor in the Department of Fine Arts at the National Taiwan University of Science and Technology.

As Secretary-General of both the Chinese Painting Institute and the Cross-Strait Cultural and Art Foundation, Tsung-Jen Pai has significantly contributed to fostering artistic and cultural exchange across the Taiwan Strait. His work has been recognized through international exhibitions and cultural exchanges, promoting dialogue between artists from both sides.
